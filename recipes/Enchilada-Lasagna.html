Enchilada Lasagna

Description

This enchilada lasagna is a Mexican-inspired twist on lasagna made with corn tortillas, cottage cheese, and enchilada sauce, perfect for dinner or potlucks.

Enchilada Lasagne combines chilli, cheese and soft tortillas. A great slimming friendly recipe for any diet plan like Weight Watchers or counting calories!

Ingredients

  • tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 ¼ pounds ground turkey
  • 1 (28 ounce) can enchilada sauce
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes with lime juice and cilantro
  • 1 (16 ounce) package small-curd cottage cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 5 (6 inch) corn tortillas, halved
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese blend
  • cooking spray
  • 1 green onion, diced
